Built around the GregTech mod on Minecraft 1.7.10, GTNH delivers a progressive, years-long journey from primitive survival through interplanetary space exploration, with over 3500 quests guiding you every step of the way.

## Getting started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install a launcher">
    Download [Prism Launcher](https://prismlauncher.org/) or [MultiMC](https://multimc.org/) — both support one-click GTNH installation. Java 17 or 21 is required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download GTNH">
    Get the latest release from [gtnewhorizons.com/downloads](https://www.gtnewhorizons.com/downloads/) and import the pack into your launcher.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the questbook">
    Once in-game, press **B** to open Better Questing. Start with the **Introduction** tab for guided first steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join the community">
    Join the [GTNH Discord](https://discord.gg/gtnh) to find server listings, ask questions, and follow development.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  GT New Horizons is designed for experienced Minecraft players. If you are new to modded Minecraft, the questbook will guide you, but expect a significant learning curve — especially in the first few hours.
</Note>
