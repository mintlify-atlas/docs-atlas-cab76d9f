Beyond the official servers, a vibrant network of player-run GTNH servers operates around the world. Community servers range from small private groups to large open servers with hundreds of registered players, covering Europe, North America, and beyond. Each server has its own rules, culture, and administration team.

<Warning>
  Community servers are not officially affiliated with the GTNH team. Rules and moderation vary by server.
</Warning>

## Joining a community server

Most community servers have their own Discord server where you can read the rules, submit a whitelist application if required, and connect with the player community before you start. Check the GTNH Discord `#server-listings` channel for links to individual server Discords and any updated connection details.

<Tip>
  Join the server's own Discord before connecting — it is the fastest way to get whitelisted, ask questions, and find players to team up with.
</Tip>
